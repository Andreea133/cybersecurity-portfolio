# 🎓 Bachelor's Thesis — Securing Smart Medical Devices

Bachelor's thesis (Politehnica University of Bucharest, Faculty of Electrical Engineering): **analysis and protection of smart medical (IoT) devices against cyber attacks.**

The thesis includes a personal technical contribution — an **email-based Multi-Factor Authentication (OTP) system** — plus encryption for protecting patient data, threat scenarios, and solution testing.
