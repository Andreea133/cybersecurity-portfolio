# Cybersecurity Risk Assessment (Medical Clinic)

> Part of the **GoIT Cybersecurity Specialist** program (2024–2025).

## Objective
Assess cyber risks to the digital assets of a medical clinic, classify assets by criticality, evaluate exposure, and propose mitigations.

## Tools & Technologies
Risk assessment, asset classification, impact analysis

## Skills Demonstrated
Risk management, business-impact analysis

## Outcome
A full risk assessment with asset ratings and prioritized controls.
